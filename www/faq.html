What is CamPay?

CamPay is a mobile app that enables you to pay or receive funds offline. It is a credit card free app and works offline by just scanning a qrcode of user who wishes to receive funds. Although qr code is the main technology, entering the designated account details manually is still supported.


How Does It Work?

CamPay works using the underlying technology of bank USSD codes for transactions. What it does is to extract key ussd code and parameter informations formatted into USSD code and sends to phone dialer to complete transaction


How Do I Make Transactions?

Receive

Click on receive tab.
Select account you wish to receive funds to.
If none exists, create one under settings tab.
Enter amount.
Click on ok to generate qr code.

Send

Click on scan qr code to scan a generated code or enter values manually.
Click on ok button.


What is needed to use CamPay?

An android or iOS powered device.
A registered sim linked to bank account you wish to receive or send money from.
N.B sim must be active and linked to your bank account with a secure pin for carrying out USSD code bank transactions

What Banks are enabled?

CamPay currently supports Access Bank, Diamond Bank, Fidelity, FirstBank, Heritage Bank, Keystone Bank, GTBank, Sterling Bank, Wema, Zenith. This may change in the future

Having issues scanning QrCode

Reduce screen brightness, so the camera can see the qrcode clearly.

App crashes when QrCode Scanner is launched

App requires permission to access camera on your device in other to scan qrcode. Please allow camera permissions from system settings or during download.
